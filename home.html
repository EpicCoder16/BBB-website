Our Mission:

Breaking Barriers in Business is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit which provides the opportunity for students to learn more about having a career in business who may not have that chance at their schools. We look forward to creating a community of students who not only aspire to have a career in business, but also share our vision and commitment to advocating for fair and equal treatment of all people in the business sector, regardless of their race, gender, religion, or any other characteristic.
